GROOVE FITNESS — PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Groove Fitness / Mogil Software

1. Overview

Groove Fitness ("the App") is a fitness and workout planning application designed to help users track workouts, generate personalized training plans, and monitor training progress.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing personal data responsibly and transparently.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, how it is shared, and the rights you have as a user.

If you have questions, you may contact us at: support@groove.fitness

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

We use Sign in with Apple to authenticate users. Apple provides us with:

  • a unique, anonymous Apple user identifier
  • optionally, a user-selected display name (not legal name)

We do not collect legal names, email addresses, or other contact details from Apple.

2.2 Profile Information

Users may choose to provide:

  • display name
  • age
  • birthday
  • training experience
  • fitness goals and goal datapoints
  • favorite exercises
  • interests related to training
  • desired number of workouts per week

This information is used only for personalization and workout recommendations.

2.3 Workout Data

We collect workout-related data entered or generated by the user, including:

  • individual workouts
  • exercises performed
  • sets, reps, and weights
  • difficulty or effort ratings
  • workout summaries
  • muscle soreness reports
  • feedback on exercises or workouts

This data is used to track progress and generate future workouts.

2.4 Technical Data

We do not collect:

  • device model
  • operating system version
  • IP addresses
  • advertising identifiers
  • location data
  • contacts, photos, or any personal files

The API backend logs non-identifying error information for debugging, but no logs are tied to a user ID or personal identifier.

3. How Data Is Stored

3.1 On-Device Storage

Some data is stored locally on the user’s device to support offline access.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Backup & Sync)

Workout data and profile data are synced to:

  • Google Firestore, hosted on Google Cloud Platform (GCP)

Firestore acts as a secure encrypted backup and allows syncing across devices.

Our backend API is hosted on Fly.io, which processes data securely via encrypted connections.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data only for these purposes:

  • Workout generation and personalization: Using your workout history and profile data to create training plans.
  • Progress tracking: Displaying historical workout performance, rep schemes, and soreness trends.
  • Backup and sync: Ensuring your data is preserved across devices.
  • Error handling: Non-identifying server error logs may be used to troubleshoot issues.

We do not use personal data for:

  • marketing
  • advertising
  • tracking across apps or websites
  • profiling beyond workout personalization

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Firestore (GCP)

Used for secure data storage and sync. Receives only the data necessary to store user profile and workout information.

5.2 Google Gemini (AI Services)

We use the Gemini API to generate workout plans, evaluate training progress, and enhance profile recommendations.

Data sent to Gemini includes:

  • exercises performed
  • sets/reps/weights
  • difficulty ratings
  • soreness and workout feedback
  • goals
  • age
  • training experience
  • favorite exercises
  • interests
  • desired workout frequency
  • user display name only (never full name or email)

We never send legal names, emails, account identifiers, or any data that can personally identify a user.

Gemini processes data only to fulfill the request and does not retain or use the information for training unless explicitly permitted by you (we do not permit this).

5.3 No Advertising or Tracking Services

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

We do not use analytics SDKs, tracking frameworks, or ad networks.

6. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell personal data.

We do not share data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

We share data only with:

  • Firestore (for storage)
  • Gemini (to generate workouts)

Both are considered data processors, not data purchasers.

7. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as the user maintains an active account.

When a user chooses to delete their account, all associated personal data is permanently removed from our servers and Firestore backups within 3 days.

8. User Rights

8.1 Access

Users may view all data inside the Groove Fitness app.

8.2 Correction

Users may update or change their profile data at any time.

8.3 Deletion

Users may request account deletion from inside the app. All data is permanently deleted within 3 days.

8.4 Export

Users may request an export of their data by contacting support.

9. Children’s Privacy

Groove Fitness is not intended for users under 13.

We do not knowingly collect information from children.

If we learn that a child has created an account, we will delete the data immediately.

10. Security

We protect user data through:

  • encrypted network connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • encrypted storage via Firestore’s encryption mechanisms
  • restricted server access
  • no plaintext passwords stored (authentication handled by Apple)

Despite best efforts, no system is perfectly secure.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically.

Continued use of the app after changes constitute acceptance of the updated policy.
